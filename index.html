The Complete Fiddle Tunes I either did or did not learn at the Tractor Tavern

by Gene Silberberg

This collection contains the tunes from the original 'Fiddle Tunes I Learned at the Tractor Tavern' and '93 Tunes I Didn't Learn at the Tracor Tavern' plus a few extra strays. I wrote these tunes out because that was the only way I knew to remember and keep track of them.
